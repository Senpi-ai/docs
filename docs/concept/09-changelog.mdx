- [Changes in Protocol Fees](./03-moxie-token.mdx)

  - Removed all fees on Buy transactions of Fan Tokens

  - 5% fee on Sell transactions of Fan Tokens split between protocol and Subject whose Fan Tokens are being sold

    - 50% of the fee (5% x 50% = 2.5%) is distributed to the Subject whose Fan Tokens are being sold

    - 50% of the fee (5% x 50% = 2.5%) is in MOXIE collected by the protocol and currently set to be burned.
