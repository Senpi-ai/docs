# Moxie Earn Developer Preview

**Moxie Earn is a complete onchain advertising platform for Farcaster, powered by Moxie!**

Moxie Earn **helps advertisers get more high quality engagement** -- great for product launches, feature releases, user acquisition, brand activations, retention, increasing awareness, broadening your reach and more!

Moxie Earn **enables Farcaster members to earn more Moxie** for themselves and their fans -- by amplifying content they like.

Moxie Earn **enables Farcaster devs to integrate high-value** feeds and boosted earnings opportunities into their apps and frames.

Moxie Earn is scheduled to launch on Thursday, 7th November 2024.

To use Moxie Earn, make sure to install the [Moxie Cast action](https://warpcast.com/~/add-cast-action?url=https%3A%2F%2Fmoxie-frames.airstack.xyz%2Fmca%2Faction) for Warpcast.
