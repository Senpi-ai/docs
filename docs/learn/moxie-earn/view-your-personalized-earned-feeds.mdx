# View Your Personalized Earned Feeds

To view your personalized earned feeds, you can simply click the `Earn` button on the Moxie Earn mini-app:

<div style={{textAlign: 'center'}}>

![earn](/img/moxie-earn/earn.png)

</div>

When clicked, you will be able to view a list Moxie Earn promotions that is eligible for you.

For each promoted casts, you will have the option to either quote cast the promoted cast to earn extra rewards or simply hide them from your feeds.

<div style={{textAlign: 'center'}}>

![quote-cast](/img/moxie-earn/moxie_earn_promotions.png)

</div>
